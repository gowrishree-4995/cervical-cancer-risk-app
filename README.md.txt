# Cervical Cancer Risk Prediction App

This app uses a trained XGBoost model to assess the risk of cervical cancer based on clinical and lifestyle inputs. Built with Gradio and deployable on Hugging Face Spaces.

- ✅ Displays risk level (Low, Moderate, High)
- 📊 Shows risk percentage
- 🛡️ Gives tailored medical recommendations

Trained on UCI's Cervical Cancer dataset.
